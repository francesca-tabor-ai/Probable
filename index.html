The forecast trap

Ops leads, strategy teams, and finance leaders are stuck. You own revenue targets, resource planning, product roadmaps—but you're flying blind.

  • Single-number forecasts create false certainty. One point estimate hides the real range of outcomes and leaves you exposed when reality lands elsewhere.
  • Static snapshots go stale. By the time your next planning cycle rolls around, the data has moved and you're redoing everything by hand.
  • Scattered tools—spreadsheets, BI, separate risk models—that don't connect. Leadership asks "how confident are we?" and you're piecing together a story from five sources.
  • Gut-feel decisions when it matters most. Missed targets, misallocated resources, and slower, less confident planning.
